Privacy Policy for Code-Rhythm

Last Updated: June 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Code-Rhythm ("the App") handles information.

1. No Data Collection

Code-Rhythm is designed with your privacy in mind. This App does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data from its users.

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any other identifying information.
  • Device information (like IP address, device ID, or operating system details).
  • Usage data (like app launch times, features used, or interactions).
  • Location data.
  • Any content you generate or interact with within the app.

2. How the App Works

Code-Rhythm allows users to download publicly available source files from GitHub for the purpose of practicing typing against their text content.

  • GitHub Access: When you choose to download a file, the App connects directly to GitHub's public repositories to retrieve the selected content. This connection is solely for the purpose of downloading the requested file to your device. The App does not interact with your personal GitHub account (if you have one) or transmit any data to GitHub other than the request to download a specific public file.

3. No Third-Party Services (for Data Collection)

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services that would collect user data.

4. Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect any personal data from anyone, including children. Therefore, we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. As we do not collect any personal information, we would not have such data to remove.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
